About Us

Engineering trust, one launch at a time.

Ehtan Alax began with a simple frustration: too many businesses were being sold slow, generic websites that looked the part but did nothing for growth. We set out to build differently — every project starts with the outcome a client needs, then works backward into design, code, and content.

Today, we operate as a focused studio rather than a factory. Every site we ship is hand-built, performance-audited, and optimized for search from day one, so our clients compete online with confidence — not guesswork.

Trusted by founders, retailers, and enterprises across the UK and beyond.

Built for speed

Every site is optimized for Core Web Vitals — lean code, lazy-loaded media, and clean architecture.

Search-ready

Technical SEO, semantic markup, and structured data baked into every build, not bolted on after.

Transparent process

Fixed pricing, clear milestones, and direct access to the people building your project.

Built to grow

Scalable foundations mean your site expands with your business, not against it.
